Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process?
Our design process follows a structured approach: Discovery → Research → Design → Development → Testing → Launch. We collaborate closely with clients at each stage to ensure the final product meets their expectations and business goals.
How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A basic brand identity project typically takes 2-3 weeks, while a complete website with custom design can take 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the proposal phase.
What files will I receive upon completion?
You'll receive all necessary file formats for your project. For brand identity: vector files (AI, EPS, SVG), raster files (PNG, JPG), and brand guidelines. For websites: source files, CMS access, and documentation. We ensure you have everything needed for future use.
Do you offer ongoing support?
Yes, we offer various support packages depending on your needs. From basic maintenance to comprehensive retainer agreements that include updates, security monitoring, and ongoing design improvements. We'll recommend the best option for your business.
How do you handle revisions?
Each package includes a set number of revision rounds. We gather detailed feedback at each stage and implement changes efficiently. Additional revisions can be accommodated at our hourly rate. Clear communication ensures we get it right within the allocated revisions.

About Typixel

Founded in 2015, Typixel has grown from a small design studio into an award-winning creative agency known for pushing boundaries and delivering exceptional results. Our team of talented designers, developers, and strategists are passionate about creating digital experiences that make a difference.

We believe that great design is more than just aesthetics—it's about solving problems, telling stories, and connecting with people on an emotional level. Our client-centered approach ensures that every project we undertake is tailored to meet specific business goals while exceeding expectations.
