Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A simple logo design might take 1-2 weeks, while a complete brand identity project could take 4-6 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your design process?

Our design process typically involves: 1) Discovery and research, 2) Concept development, 3) Design refinement, 4) Final delivery, and 5) Support. We collaborate closely with you throughout the entire process to ensure your vision is realized.

How many revisions are included?

The number of revisions depends on the package you choose. Our Starter plan includes 2 revisions, Professional includes 5, and Enterprise includes unlimited revisions. Additional revisions can be purchased separately if needed.

Do you provide source files?

Yes, all our packages include the source files for your designs. This allows you to make future edits or use the designs across various platforms. We provide files in industry-standard formats suitable for both print and digital use.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For larger projects, we offer flexible payment plans with a 50% upfront payment and the remaining balance due upon project completion.
